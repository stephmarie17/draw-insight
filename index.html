a tool to integrate a bit of magic into your day

The tarot is a divination tool dating back to the fifteenth century. It is not meant to tell your fortune necessarily but instead to allow you to focus on the invisible influences in your life and to reflect. Drawing a daily card can help you answer a question, resolve an issue, or define a mood for the day.

what is the tarot?

A traditional tarot deck consists of 78 cards, consisting of the 22 cards of the Major Arcana and the 56 cards of the Minor Arcana. The Major Arcana function as trump cards, representing the Fool's Journey beginning at card 0. The Fool's Journey is an archetypal arc, with the story going that each card represents a person or a lesson that the Fool learns until they meet the World. These cards can represent a person, an event, or the querent.
